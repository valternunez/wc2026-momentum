# WC2026 Momentum — Project Audit

This is a strong, editorially mature, and unusually honest piece of causal-flavored data journalism, built on a sound, well-tested, and reproducible infrastructural spine. The windowing/clustering machinery is deterministic and correct, the local-scrape/cloud-publish split holds, raw data is gitignored, and the prose repeatedly and consistently refuses a causal headline ("suggestive, not proven"). The single biggest risk is inferential: the headline GAP (the "leftover" break effect of ~7 points) is a difference of two independently bootstrapped point estimates with **no uncertainty attached anywhere in the repo**, and when properly bootstrapped the difference crosses zero — yet the copy presents it as "a small real effect." This is compounded by a placebo-construction confound: the within-2026 control is sampled at different match-clock minutes (10/35/55/80) and different pre-momentum levels than the real breaks (~22/67), so the placebo under-controls for the very regression-to-the-mean it exists to net out. Secondary structural risks: the signed-off TWFE/momentum-killer model is never wired into the published site (and when run, is null at p≈0.58), the test suite is RED on a clean checkout, and a commentary classifier produces phantom VAR/injury stoppages that contaminate the parquet. The presentation layer is polished but has real WCAG AA contrast failures, hover-only affordances that fail on touch, and a few Spanish mistranslations. None of these break the page or the data spine; the corrective work is concentrated in (a) attaching an interval to the GAP and softening three sentences, (b) fixing placebo position/level matching, and (c) a handful of contrast/touch/i18n fixes.

## Findings by area

### Statistical inference & causal identification
*(Findings 1, 2, 3, 8, 9, 10, 11 above are the majors/blocker of this lane. Additional:)*

- **Minor — RTM conditioning is partially mechanical** (`descriptive.py:24,50`). Selecting pre>0 then differencing yields a negative mean delta under pure RTM, so raw HERO_DELTA is not interpretable alone — only the placebo-netted GAP is. *Why it matters:* raises the stakes on the GAP having a proper interval. *Rec:* lead with the netted comparison; show HERO/P26 side-by-side from the first sentence including on social cards. *Confidence: medium (statistician).* No explicit handling of pre_mean==0 ties, which silently drops balanced breaks and slightly inflates the drop magnitude — decide a documented tie rule or assert it's empty (data-scientist, medium).
- **Minor — duration & acclimatization nulls are underpowered / slope+split double-reporting** (`duration_effect.py:41`, `acclimatization.py:152`, `heat_interaction.py:80`). Each fits a slope AND a median split on the same small clustered sample; reporting both invites cherry-picking. Acclimatization "null" read as evidence of absence. *Rec:* one estimator per question (prefer continuous slope + clustered CI), phrase nulls as "no detectable association at current N," raise min-N guards. *Confidence: medium (statistician, red-team).*
- **Minor — VAR reference category + post-treatment control** (`regression.py:105-115,41`). If the FE model is ever surfaced, hydration-vs-VAR is a treatment-vs-treatment contrast (VAR is itself momentum-disruptive), not break-vs-nothing, and SCORE_CHANGING_VAR drops make the reference composition selection-dependent. *Rec:* use the quiet-minute placebo as the causal baseline, or report vs both and show agreement. *Confidence: medium (statistician).*
- **Minor — fixed-seed bootstrap MC noise** (`descriptive.py:32` seed=7; some paths n_boot=800). Reported CI is one Monte-Carlo draw; 800 reps carry non-trivial endpoint noise invisible to readers. *Rec:* keep the seed but bump reps ≥5000 where cheap; assert seed-stability in a test. *Confidence: low (statistician).*

### Feature validity & pipeline
*(Majors 2, 7, 13 above. Additional:)*

- **Minor — window boundary sample-size asymmetry** (`momentum_features.py:51-52,65-69`). Half-open windows on fractional centers can yield 5 pre / 4 post, biasing a difference-in-means under any trend. *Rec:* snap centers to integer lattice or require equal pre/post counts for the headline cut; add a test. *Confidence: medium (data-scientist).* Related: no minimum coverage beyond ≥2 points — a 2-point window contributes equally to a 5-point one, diluting quality (engineer, medium); carry per-window n into the parquet.
- **Minor — duration pairing greedy mis-match** (`stoppages.py:114-149,152-165`). `_delay_pairs` greedily assigns nearest later end in start order (not global optimum); interleaved near-simultaneous breaks can mis-pair — the same failure mode as the documented prior spurious-slope bug. *Rec:* global/optimal matching; add an interleaved-resume fixture. *Confidence: medium (data-scientist).*
- **Minor — reconcile dedup over-merging** (`reconcile.py:90-101`). A minute-less same-type line folds into the first same-type merged event regardless of time distance, potentially attaching second-half evidence to a first-half break. *Rec:* only fold when exactly one same-type candidate; add a two-event test. *Confidence: low (data-scientist).*
- **Minor — acclimatization venue-WBGT provenance asymmetry** (`acclimatization.py:67-81,128-132`). WC2026 reuses parquet WBGT while other tournaments geocode live; provenance-correlated missingness could create a spurious cross-tournament gradient in the pooled `nations_test`. *Rec:* log per-tournament resolution rates; source WBGT identically. *Confidence: low (data-scientist).*

## Cross-cutting & disagreements

**Cross-cutting themes (multiple lenses):**

1. **The GAP is asserted without an interval and likely crosses zero** — raised by the **statistician** (BLOCKER) and independently reproduced by the **red-team** (CI [−13.84, +0.67], p≈0.08). The single most important convergence in the audit; both demand the difference be bootstrapped and the three sentences (HERO/BOTTOM/S05) softened.

2. **The within-2026 placebo under-controls for RTM** — the **data-scientist** (position mismatch: 10/35/55/80 vs 22/67), **statistician** (position confound), **football** (80'/10' carry game-state regimes), and **red-team** (pre-level mismatch +28.95 vs +25.61, gap shrinks to ~−4) all hit this from different angles. Merge: match the placebo on **both** match-clock position **and** pre-momentum level.

3. **Numbers in the writeup depend on gitignored/non-deterministic inputs** — flagged by **data-scientist** (placebo parquets), **red-team** (window-robustness via `load_raw`), **devops** (weather cache, ACCL_CLUBS), and **engineer** (weather-wipe). Same CLAUDE.md hard-rule violation surfaced across four lanes; the momentum headline is reproducible, the heat/robustness/placebo numbers are not.

4. **FotMob momentum is misdescribed as an xT model** — **football** (high), **editor**, and **data-scientist** all independently caught the same factual overreach; the fix is identical (drop "expected-threat," reserve xT for the StatsBomb cross-check).

5. **The hero −24 band presents an RTM-induced/raw number as the finding without its counter-number** — **editorial** (band vs cards), **red-team** (selection-guaranteed-negative), **statistician**, and **editorial-UX** (unanchored magnitude) converge. Fix: add the counter-number to the band, mirroring the social cards.

6. **Hover/tooltip-only explainers fail on touch and for AT, hiding the RTM definition** — **mobile-UX** (no touch handlers), **a11y** (no `aria-describedby`/`aria-expanded`, 16px target), and **editorial-UX** (RTM definition hidden) all land on the same component. Single fix: promote RTM to visible copy + add touch + ARIA + 24px target.

7. **Shade-WBGT proxy biases §06 toward "breaks unnecessary"** — **football** and **red-team** (proxy under-counts hot matches in the conclusion-supporting direction) and **editor** (asymmetric certainty vs the momentum half). Merge: soften §06 and disclose the proxy under-states on-pitch heat.

8. **ES register inconsistency (tú-imperatives vs impersonal)** — raised by both the **ES-editor** and the **editorial-UX** reviewer; one entry, two lenses.

**Explicit disagreements / tensions:**

- **Is there a "real effect" at all?** The **red-team** argues the licensed claim is "no detectable difference" (GAP CI crosses zero; TWFE interaction null at p=0.58; CWC2025 club baseline equals the break number) and that the piece's last-mile rhetoric overstates a genuinely-null result. The **statistician** agrees the GAP inference is unsupported as written but frames it as fixable presentation ("soften to not-distinguishable-from-zero") on top of a fundamentally sound design, and the **football** expert and **devops** auditor both stress the infrastructure and design spine are credible. Net: there is no disagreement that the *current* GAP claim is overstated; the disagreement is one of emphasis — red-team leans toward "the headline residual is probably zero," statistician/football toward "the design is right, only the uncertainty quantification and three sentences need fixing." Both views are preserved; neither should be silently resolved before the difference-CI is actually computed.
- **Which no-break baseline is canonical?** The **red-team** holds that CWC2025 (closest like-conditions comparator) yielding ~zero gap is convenient to set aside; the project's framing (and implicitly the football lens, on physiological/sample grounds) treats clubs as a different regime. Unresolved — recommendation is to show the gap vs **all** baselines as a range rather than narrate one away.
- **TWFE: surface it or not?** The **statistician** offers two acceptable resolutions (wire it in and report the null, OR change the copy to admit it's a descriptive mean); the **red-team** is firmer that the null must be reported because the copy currently implies the test merely awaits more data. Both agree the status quo (describe a model never run) is wrong.
